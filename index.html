Welcome

Imagine cruising along beautiful Lake Michigan with friends, fresh air, and your favorite cannabis in a safe, licensed lounge. Smoke on the Water Michigan offers a unique, upscale cruise experience where adults can unwind and enjoy cannabis in a controlled environment.
